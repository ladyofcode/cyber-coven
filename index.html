Cyber Coven

A tight-knit community of like-minded womxn in the tech industry who value genuine friendship and unity.

How we do things

Most of our interaction surrounds regular video calls where we share our direct experience and chat about mental health. We foster support between womxn of different backgrounds and careers in tech.

What we believe in

We like to celebrate you being you. We value progress, and openly discuss our collective experience of being human in a way we may not share publicly.

Joining us

Anyone who is open-minded, accepting, empathetic, and is LGBTQ+ ally, is welcome to join us.

If that sounds like you, you're welcome to shoot Zahra a DM on Twitter.
